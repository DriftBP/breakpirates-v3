Welcome to Break Pirates

Established back in 2001, Break Pirates broadcast the very best in underground breakbeat oriented music live over the Internet every day of the week. From the latest Hardcore, Drum & Bass and Dubstep tunes to the Oldskool House, Hardcore, and Jungle classics, you'll find them all here. The station unites DJs worldwide, giving a wider view of the music scene.
