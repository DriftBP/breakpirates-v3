Mobile

Introduction

It's possible to tune into Break Pirates wherever you might be, including in the car, on the bus, on a train, or just walking around town. It's easier than you think, so here's a quick guide to get you locked in!

One quick warning before I move on...listening to Internet radio through your phone involves downloading a large amount of data. Typically a one hour show will be around 60MB, so please ensure that you either have a data allowance included in your call plan or you understand how much this might cost. Make sure you're aware of this before continuing!

Mobiles running Android

Here's a few apps to look for in the Android Market:

  • TuneIn Radio
  • XiiaLive

iPhone, iPad, and iPod Touch

Here's a few apps to look for in the App Store:

  • TuneIn Radio

Stream URL

Enter the following URL into your radio player:

http://bpstream.hostco.de:3000/listen.pls

In the car

Of course using the above instructions you can also tune in while driving, either using Bluetooth, aux input or a mini FM transmitter which will allow you to listen through your car speakers. If you need a transmitter these can be picked up on Ebay and aren't that expensive.
